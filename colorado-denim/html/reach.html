Reach
─────

Every year on Earth Day, Colorado Denim contributes its charitable donations to nonprofits that focus on clean water supplies. Unlike Colorado Denim, some unethical fast-fashion companies carry out their dying process in countries where there are no environmental regulations. These dyes in the textile making process are polluting water supplies. We hope that these funds will reduce the carbon footprint of our fellow companies in the fashion industry.
